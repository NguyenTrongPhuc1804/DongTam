CERAMIC & PORCELAIN TILES

By employing scientific and technical developments, constantly improving production line with the European technology, our ceramic and granite tiles have a variety of sizes and eco-friendly features. Most notably, we are selling large size tiles from 80x80cm to 100x100cm with new features such as diamond light effects, anti corrosion and sliding techniques R12, S3D technology with authentic images, and fermentation techinques with antibacterial capacity of up-to-99%, certified to be safe for consumer health by Pasteur Institute in HCMC.

Each of our products always meets the most stringent demand and is exported to other strict markets such as Japan, U.S, Singapore,…
